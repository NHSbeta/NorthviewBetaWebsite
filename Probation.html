RULES ABOUT GETTING HOURS

Beta Club is an environment where service, character, and integrity thrive. Beta Board will NOT tolerate any form of horseplaying, teasing, or stealing of any sort at ANY Beta Event. When volunteering, Beta members must be on top of their tasks and not fiddle around on their phones. It is imperative that all Beta members are on their best behavior at all times. The quality of the behavior will impact the number of hours the members will receive and potentially lead them to be on probation.

HOUR REQUIREMENTS

Each semester, members must complete a total of 10 hours, and at least 5 of those hours must be BETA SPONSORED. The email will indicate clearly which volunteer event is Beta Sponsored. Northview Beta Club hosts numerous volunteer events and opportunities, and members will not have any excuse if they fail to fulfill the required amount of hours. Failure to do so will lead to probation. 

** Beta members must fulfill their summer and fall hours to attend Beta Convention in January. Any member on probation will not be permitted to attend.

PROBATION

If members do not fulfill their hour requirement or the attendance, members are put on probation and must make up the hours the following semester. Failure to do so will result in immediate termination Beta membership.

HOW TO GET OUT OF PROBATION

For every meeting missed over the 1 meeting limit, members must make up one regular hour the following semester.

If members cancel their position at a volunteer event and fail to excuse the absence over three times, members must make up the equated sponsored hours offered in the event.

If you were on probation during summer (meaning you did not fulfill requirements during the spring), you may fulfill the probation requirements with regular hours only (since Northview Beta Club does NOT sponsored hours over summer).
